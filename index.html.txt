  <h1>Role Mapping Guide</h1>
  <p>Role mapping is the process of defining job roles within an organization and linking them to specific skills, responsibilities, and goals. It ensures clarity, reduces confusion, and aligns employeesâ€™ work with company objectives.</p>

  <h2>Why Role Mapping Matters</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Aligns talent with business objectives</li>
    <li>Improves hiring and training</li>
    <li>Boosts productivity and reduces overlap</li>
    <li>Supports succession planning</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Steps to Create a Role Map</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>Define organizational goals</li>
    <li>List all roles and responsibilities</li>
    <li>Match roles to required skills</li>
    <li>Check for gaps and overlaps</li>
    <li>Update regularly</li>
  </ol>
